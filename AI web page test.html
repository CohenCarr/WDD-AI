What is Pi?

Pi (π) is a mathematical constant representing the ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter. It is approximately equal to 3.14159 and is an irrational number, meaning its decimal representation goes on infinitely without repeating.

Pi Pie

To celebrate Pi Day on March 14th (3/14), many people enjoy baking and eating pies. This is a fun way to combine the mathematical concept of Pi with the deliciousness of pie!
